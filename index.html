Ensign Frequency Adventure

About

The bridge crew simulator Ensign Adventure puts you in the perspective of Ensign Brazoxa aboard the USS Quack. You are under attack by the Blorg.

The tactical officer, captain and ship engineer talk with each other and Brazoxa, intensifying the critical situation, that only YOU can master using arrow, WASD or game pad input controls!

Ensign Adventure was created at Berlin Mini Jam using LÖVE 2D as a parody of Star Trek.

Download

ensign-adventure-1.0.love [mirror] - You need Love2d to play (runs on Linux, Mac OS X and Windows).
